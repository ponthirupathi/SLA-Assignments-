OrderList For Cooking My Favorite Dish BRINJAL CURRY....

    Steps :


    1. Prepare the brinjals :

      • Wash and cut the brinjals into cubes.
      • To prevent discoloration and reduce bitterness, soak them in salted water briefly (a few minutes)

    2. Saute the tempering :

      • Heat oil in a pan.
      • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds; let them splutter
      • Add curry leaves.

    3. Cook onions, garlic, ginger :

      • Add chopped onion (and green chili) and sauté till onions turn translucent/golden.
      • Add ginger + garlic (or paste) and sauté until raw smell disappears.

    4. Add tomato and spices :

      • Add chopped tomato and a pinch of salt, cook till tomatoes soften.
      • Add turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well and cook for a minute until the raw smell of spices is gone.

    5. Add brinjals and water :

      • Drain the brinjals from the soaking water, then add them to the pan.
      • Stir gently to coat them with the masala.
      • Add about ½ cup water (or more, depending on how much gravy you want).

    6. Simmer:

      • Cover and let it cook over low to medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the brinjals are soft and cooked through.
      • the curry becomes too dry, you can sprinkle a little water..

    7. Final touches :

      • Once cooked, check salt and adjust.
      • Garnish with coriander leaves.

    8. Serve:

      • Serve hot with rice, roti, or any Indian bread of your choice.

      Finally My Favorite Dish Ready....
